Pasta Recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 serving of Walmart Pasta
  • 1/4 can of Walmart tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add pasta to the pot
  3. Cook until your preferred aldenteness which is usually around 10 minutes. Be sure to stir the pot.
  4. Garnish with almost anything you have since you're a broke college student
