Ramen Recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of Shin Ramen
  • Spam
  • Gochujang
  • Sausage
  • Lettuce
  • Eggs
  • Cilantro
  • Sesame seeds
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add sliced Spam and Sausage and Lettuce and Mushrooms into the pot
  3. Cook for 2 minutes
  4. Add ramen packs and seasoning to the pot
  5. Cook until your preferred aldenteness
  6. Garnish with Sesame Seeds and Cilnatro and serve
